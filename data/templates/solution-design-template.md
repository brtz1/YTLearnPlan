# Solution Design Template

## Request

Summarize the problem in plain language.

## Assumptions

List the assumptions that shape the design.

## YouTrack Configuration

What projects, fields, states, boards, or permissions are required?

## Workflow/App/API Components

What belongs in configuration, workflow scripts, apps, or external
integrations?

## Trade-offs

Option A and Option B with practical pros and cons.

## Risks

Where can the design break or create maintenance cost?

## Maintenance Burden

Who owns the automation and what changes will be frequent?
